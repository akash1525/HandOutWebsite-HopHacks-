Lend a hand. See the difference.

HandOut connects the medically ailing population of Baltimore to people who want to help.


How can HandOut help sick people ask for aid?

After registering with HandOut, people who are sick can upload a video describing their needs, from money for medicine to free clothes. Benefactors can then view these videos and donate.

What can someone who wants to help do using HandOut?

HandOut emphasizes local impact. Benefactors can only see the profiles of sick people within a 10-mile radius, allowing benefactors to provide direct services, not just money.

Donate Money

View different profiles, and choose who you want to help! Donate money so that people in need can buy medicine, visit the doctor, and more.

Volunteer

Volunteer to assist an elderly, handicapped woman who needs help moving around the house, or volunteer to be a personal nurse to a veteran! There's much you can do.

Provide Necessities

Many sick people can't afford necessities due to the heavy price of medicine and medical care. You can help by providing clothes, food, water, and much more!
